## 1. The Problem (1 slide)

- **AI Hype vs. Reality:** AI is powerful, but applications are often black boxes, prone to hallucinations, and lack auditable control.
- **Current Gap:** Businesses struggle with AI governance, compliance (especially regulated industries), data privacy, and ensuring AI decisions are accurate and traceable.
- **Risk:** Uncontrolled AI leads to factual errors, legal issues, data breaches, and wasted resources.

---

## 2. Our Solution: Garvis (1-2 slides)

- **Garvis is an AI Governance Layer:** We provide a robust framework to make AI systems truthful, auditable, and controllable.
- **Core Tenets:**
    - **Truthfulness & Provenance:** Every data point and AI output is traced to its source.
    - **Immutable Auditability:** All actions logged, ensuring complete traceability.
    -   **Explainable AI:** AI outputs detail sources, confidence, and reasoning.
    -   **Controlled Access:** Strict policies (CAC) prevent data leaks across AKBs.
    -   **Human-in-the-Loop:** Critical actions require explicit approval.
-   **Key Components:**
    -   **AKBs (Authoritative Knowledge Bundles):** Siloed, structured data.
    -   **Governance Engine:** Policy evaluation, action orchestration.
    -   **Pig Pen Adapters:** Orchestrates multiple LLMs.
    -   **Audit Trail:** Verifiable, append-only logs.
    -   **Pearl & Pig Branding:** Trust-centric, user-friendly interface.
